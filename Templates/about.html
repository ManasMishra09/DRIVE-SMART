About Pathhole Tracker!


Welcome!

This website allows you to detect and report potholes in your community using the YOLO model.

How It Works

1. Upload images containing potholes.

2. YOLO model analyzes the images for potholes.

3. Results are displayed, showing the identified potholes.

Reporting Potholes

Help improve road safety by reporting potholes.
